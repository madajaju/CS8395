# Week 14: Synthesis and Final Defense

**Purpose:** This final week brings together all the patterns, tradeoffs, and principles learned throughout the semester to prepare students for real-world architecture defense and failure analysis.

---

## Lectures

1.  **[Failure Postmortems](./failure-postmortems/README.md)**
    *   Learn the art of "Blameless Postmortems" and how to identify root causes in complex distributed systems.
2.  **[Tradeoff Narratives](./tradeoff-narratives/README.md)**
    *   Master the ability to explain *why* a specific architecture was chosen, including the "losing" options that were considered.
3.  **[Architecture Defense Prep](./architecture-defense-prep/README.md)**
    *   Practical techniques for presenting and defending a system design to stakeholders and technical peers.
4.  **[Industry Patterns](./industry-patterns/README.md)**
    *   A review of how major tech companies (Netflix, Uber, Amazon) apply these patterns at extreme scale.

---

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this week, students will be able to:
- Conduct a blameless postmortem for a multi-service outage.
- Construct a compelling tradeoff narrative that justifies architectural decisions.
- Defend a complex system design against common technical critiques.
- Recognize common "anti-patterns" in distributed system design.
